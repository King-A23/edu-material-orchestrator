# Handout Quality Rubric

## Coverage

- Does the handout cover the major topics from the source?
- Are omissions called out as unresolved items when coverage is incomplete?

## Duplication

- Are repeated titles, explanations, or examples merged cleanly?
- Is the duplicate rate low enough for a lecture handout?

## Provenance

- Do sections retain `source_refs`?
- Are figure notes and image references traceable back to source units?

## Coherence

- Does the handout read like a teacher-facing or learner-facing explanation rather than a raw dump?
- Are transitions between sections understandable?

## Low-confidence handling

- Are OCR issues or missing render steps surfaced explicitly?
- Does QA make unresolved content visible before delivery?
